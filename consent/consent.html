tempy.email

Data transmission consent

To generate disposable inboxes and show messages, this extension needs to send limited data to tempy.email.

  • Data sent to tempy.email Mailbox creation requests and the generated mailbox address when fetching messages.
  • Stored locally Your settings and most recent generated address.
  • Not collected Browsing history, search terms, analytics, or tracking data.

If you decline, the extension cannot generate inboxes or fetch messages.
